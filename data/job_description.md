# Job Description: Senior AI Engineer — Founding Team

**Company:** Redrob AI (Series A AI-native talent intelligence platform)
**Location:** Pune/Noida, India (Hybrid — flexible cadence) | Open to relocation candidates from Tier-1 Indian cities
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Experience Required:** 5–9 years

## About the Role
We are building a new AI Engineering organization from scratch. Your mandate is to own the intelligence layer of Redrob's product. You will build and scale the ranking, retrieval, and matching systems that connect recruiters with candidates. 

We need an engineer with deep technical depth in modern ML systems (embeddings, retrieval, ranking, LLMs, fine-tuning) paired with a scrappy product-engineering attitude—someone who favors shipping, learning from real users, and iterating over theoretical perfection.

### Your First 90 Days
* **Weeks 1-3:** Audit our current systems (BM25 + rule-based scoring). Identify the 3-4 highest-leverage areas for improvement.
* **Weeks 4-8:** Ship a v2 ranking system that demonstrably improves recruiter-engagement metrics, utilizing embeddings, hybrid retrieval, and LLM-based re-ranking.
* **Weeks 9-12:** Set up the evaluation infrastructure (offline benchmarks, online A/B testing, recruiter-feedback loops) to ensure continuous, data-driven improvement.

### Long-Term Responsibilities
* Drive the long-term architecture of how we handle candidate-JD matching at scale.
* Mentor the next round of engineering hires as we grow the team.
* Work closely with product management on recruiter-experience features.

## Requirements
* **Experience:** 5–9 years total experience, with 4–5 years in applied ML/AI roles at product companies.
* **Track Record:** Have shipped at least one end-to-end ranking, search, or recommendation system to real users at meaningful scale.
* **Embeddings in Production:** Production experience with embeddings-based retrieval systems (handling embedding drift, index refresh, retrieval-quality regression).
* **Vector/Search Infrastructure:** Production experience with vector databases or hybrid search infrastructure (e.g., Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, Milvus, OpenSearch, Elasticsearch, FAISS).
* **Evaluation Frameworks:** Hands-on experience designing rigorous evaluation frameworks for ranking systems (NDCG, MRR, MAP, offline-to-online correlation, A/B testing).
* **Engineering Skills:** Strong Python skills and a commitment to high-quality production code.

## Preferred Qualifications
* LLM fine-tuning experience (LoRA, QLoRA, PEFT).
* Experience with learning-to-rank models (XGBoost-based or neural).
* Prior exposure to HR-tech, recruiting tech, or marketplace products.
* Background in distributed systems or large-scale inference optimization.
* Open-source contributions in the AI/ML space.

## Disqualifiers (Who this role is NOT for)
* Pure research backgrounds (academic or research-only) without production deployment experience.
* Candidates whose "AI experience" consists solely of recent framework wrappers (e.g., LangChain) without fundamental pre-LLM-era ML production experience.
* Senior engineers or architects who have not written production code in the last 18 months.
* Candidates whose entire career history is strictly at consulting/services firms without any product-company experience.
* Primary expertise strictly in computer vision, speech, or robotics without significant NLP/IR exposure.

## Logistics & Culture
* **Location:** Pune or Noida preferred (offices mostly used Tue/Thu). Open to candidates in Hyderabad, Mumbai, Delhi NCR willing to relocate.
* **Notice Period:** Prefer under 30 days (we can buy out up to 30 days).
* **Culture:** Async-first, writing-heavy, transparent, and fast-paced. We value system-level thinking and long-term commitment.
