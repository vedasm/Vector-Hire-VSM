# Job Description: Senior AI Engineer — Founding Team

**Company:** Redrob AI (Series A AI-native talent intelligence platform)\
**Location:** Pune/Noida, India (Hybrid, flexible cadence) | Open to relocation candidates from Tier-1 Indian cities\
**Employment Type:** Full-time\
**Experience Required:** Typically 5–9 years (strong candidates outside this range may also be considered)

---

## Role Overview

Redrob AI is building a new AI Engineering organization from scratch. The selected candidate will own the intelligence layer of the platform, including candidate-job matching, search, retrieval, ranking, recommendation, and recruiter-facing discovery systems.

The role requires a combination of:

* Deep technical expertise in modern ML systems
* Strong software engineering ability
* Product-oriented decision making
* Rapid experimentation and execution

The ideal candidate is comfortable shipping practical solutions quickly, measuring real-world impact, and iterating based on user feedback.

---

## First 90 Days

### Weeks 1–3

* Audit existing search and matching systems (currently BM25 + rule-based scoring).
* Identify highest-impact opportunities for improvement.

### Weeks 4–8

* Design and deploy a next-generation ranking system.
* Improve recruiter engagement metrics.
* Implement embeddings-based retrieval, hybrid search, and LLM-assisted re-ranking where appropriate.

### Weeks 9–12

* Build evaluation infrastructure.
* Establish offline benchmarks.
* Implement online A/B testing.
* Create recruiter feedback loops.
* Measure offline-to-online performance correlation.

---

## Long-Term Responsibilities

* Own candidate-JD matching architecture.
* Design scalable retrieval and ranking systems.
* Improve search relevance and recommendation quality.
* Mentor future engineering hires.
* Collaborate closely with product and recruiter-experience teams.
* Drive long-term AI platform strategy.

## Explicit Disqualifiers

### Research-Only Background

* Academic or research experience without production deployment.

### Wrapper-Only AI Experience

* Primarily LangChain or framework-based development without deep ML, retrieval, ranking, or production AI experience.

### Non-Coding Senior Engineers

* Senior engineers, architects, or leads who have not written production code in the last 18 months.

### Consulting-Only Careers

* Entire career spent exclusively in consulting/services companies without product-company experience.

### Wrong Domain Specialization

Primary expertise exclusively in:

* Computer Vision
* Speech
* Robotics

without significant NLP, Search, Retrieval, Ranking, or Information Retrieval experience.

### Framework-Centric Profiles

* Strong focus on tools and frameworks without evidence of system-level thinking.

### Excessive Job Hopping

* Repeated company changes approximately every 1–1.5 years without evidence of long-term ownership.

### No External Validation

* Entire career spent on closed proprietary systems with no visible technical contributions, publications, talks, or open-source work.
